Welcome to Rishikesh

Rishikesh is a spiritual town located on the banks of the Ganges River, known for its serene temples, yoga retreats, and outdoor adventures. It offers a perfect blend of peace and adventure for all kinds of travelers.

Top Attractions in Rishikesh

  • Triveni Ghat - Famous for the evening Ganga Aarti.
  • Parmarth Niketan - Renowned for yoga and spiritual retreats.
  • Ram Jhula - Iconic iron bridge across the Ganges River.
  • Neelkanth Mahadev Temple - A sacred temple amidst the mountains.
  • White Water Rafting - Adventure on the Ganges River.
