Welcome to New Delhi

New Delhi, the vibrant capital of India, is a blend of history, culture, and modernity. From ancient monuments to bustling markets, it's a city that offers something for everyone.

Top Attractions in New Delhi

  • Red Fort - A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this Mughal-era fort is a must-see for history enthusiasts.
  • India Gate - A war memorial that stands as a tribute to Indian soldiers who died during World War I.
  • Qutub Minar - The tallest brick minaret in the world, it’s an architectural marvel.
  • Lotus Temple - A Bahá'í House of Worship known for its unique lotus-shaped structure.
  • Humayun's Tomb - The tomb of Mughal Emperor Humayun, a precursor to the Taj Mahal.
