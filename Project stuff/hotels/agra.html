Welcome to Agra

Agra, home to the magnificent Taj Mahal, is one of India’s most famous tourist destinations. The city is rich in history, offering incredible Mughal architecture and a vibrant culture.

Top Attractions in Agra

  • Taj Mahal - The world-famous symbol of love and one of the Seven Wonders of the World.
  • Agra Fort - A majestic fort with rich Mughal history, offering incredible views of the Taj Mahal.
  • Fatehpur Sikri - A UNESCO World Heritage site with a fascinating mix of Indian and Mughal architecture.
  • Itmad-ud-Daula’s Tomb - Known as the "Baby Taj", it is an exquisite example of Mughal architecture.
  • Mehtab Bagh - A beautiful garden offering stunning sunset views of the Taj Mahal from across the Yamuna River.
