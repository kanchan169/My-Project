Welcome to Chitwan

Chitwan is a mesmerizing destination in Nepal, renowned for its lush jungles, exotic wildlife, and serene landscapes. It's the perfect getaway for nature lovers and adventurers alike.

Top Attractions in Chitwan

  • Chitwan National Park - A UNESCO World Heritage Site, home to rhinos, tigers, and elephants.
  • Jungle Safari - Experience the thrill of exploring the wilderness.
  • Tharu Village - Learn about the culture and traditions of the Tharu people.
  • Canoeing in Rapti River - Spot crocodiles and water birds in their natural habitat.
  • Elephant Breeding Center - Witness the conservation efforts for Asian elephants.
