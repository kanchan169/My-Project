Welcome to Goa

Goa, known for its stunning beaches, vibrant nightlife, and Portuguese heritage, offers a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure. Explore the beaches, visit forts, and indulge in delicious seafood.

Top Attractions in Goa

  • Calangute Beach - The largest beach in Goa, perfect for water sports and relaxation.
  • Fort Aguada - A 17th-century Portuguese fort with panoramic views of the Arabian Sea.
  • Se Cathedral - A magnificent example of Portuguese colonial architecture.
  • Dudhsagar Waterfalls - A breathtaking waterfall set amidst the lush forests of the Western Ghats.
  • Basilica of Bom Jesus - A UNESCO World Heritage site that houses the remains of St. Francis Xavier.
