Welcome to Sikkim

Sikkim is a stunning destination in northeastern India, known for its majestic Himalayan views, rich cultural heritage, and scenic landscapes. A perfect place for adventure and tranquility.

Top Attractions in Sikkim

  • Kanchenjunga National Park - A UNESCO World Heritage Site with stunning views of Kanchenjunga.
  • Gurudongmar Lake - One of the highest lakes in the world, located amidst the snow-covered mountains.
  • Rumtek Monastery - A significant Buddhist monastery with beautiful architecture.
  • Tsongmo Lake - A scenic lake surrounded by snow-capped mountains, offering a peaceful environment.
  • Namchi - A town known for its temples and beautiful views of the mountains.
